# **💙 Problem Statement & Brand Story**

## **Traveling Should Be About Discovering, Not Getting Lost.**

Every year, thousands of international visitors come to Chuncheon to experience its lakes, food, culture, and famous attractions.

Unfortunately, many leave without experiencing everything the city has to offer.

Why?

* Transportation is confusing for first-time visitors.  
* Information is often only available in Korean.  
* Tourist attractions are spread across the city.  
* Taxi fares become expensive.  
* Lack of knowledge on all that the city has to offer.

Instead of creating memories, travelers spend their time planning routes, waiting for buses, and worrying about getting lost and scammed.

We believe exploring a city should feel exciting—not overwhelming.

That's why we created the **Global Travel System (GTS)**.

Our mission is simple:

> **Make Chuncheon easy to explore for everyone, regardless of language or travel experience interests.**
